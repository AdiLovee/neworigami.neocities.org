Welcome to the new Origami.com

A website by Adinah Levi

NewOrigami is the spiritual successor to Origami.com, formerly by Alex Barber.

This is an archive dedicated to preserving and sharing as many publicly-available origami diagrams as possible, with a heavy focus on diagrams shared on defunct sites or found on the Wayback Machine.

In the future I would like to turn this into a giant hub of free diagrams contributed by the community.

Have a diagram you'd like to be shared here? Just upload it to any file transfer site and comment the link!
Have a creator you'd like to share? Comment their site and/or YouTube Channel and I'll also add it!

All diagrams are presented in PDF format.

Currently Hosting: 169 Diagrams

This site is regularly being updated daily at 0:00 EST, please follow the site and leave feedback and comments in the guestbook.
